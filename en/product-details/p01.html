Cathode stations
inverter protection
automatic SCZI

SCZI are cathodic protection inverter converters,
designed to protect underground metal structures from corrosion.

They are used for single and multi-threaded oil pipelines, gas pipelines, pumping and compressor stations, heating networks, electrical and water supply networks, as well as industrial facilities
