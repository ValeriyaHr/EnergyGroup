Induced Current
Diversion System
KVNS.PEG

When underground pipelines intersect or run parallel to overhead power
transmission lines, electromagnetic induction generates induced currents that
can cause intensive electrolytic corrosion of the pipeline.

The KVNS.PEG induced current diversion system is designed to protect pipelines by diverting (draining) alternating current induced by power transmission lines into a grounding system, while simultaneously maintaining the required protective potential of the pipeline structure.

System Composition
  • Test and measurement point (TMP) stand
  • Alternating current
    diversion unit
  • Lightning protection unit
  • Combined grounding electrode with connection cable
Purpose
  • Protection of metal
    structures by diverting
    power line–induced current
    into a grounding system
  • Maintaining the protective
    potential of the pipeline structure
Applications
  • Main and
    distribution pipelines
  • Pipeline sections located
    near or crossing high-voltage
    power transmission lines
  • Areas with increased
    electromagnetic influence
