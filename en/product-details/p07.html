Discharge complex
of the induced current
KVNS.PEG

When crossing or parallel laying of power transmission lines with underground
induced currents arise in the pipeline under the influence of electromagnetic induction, which, in their
in turn, cause intense electrolytic corrosion of the pipeline.

The induced current removal complex (KVNS.PEG) is designed to protect pipelines
by diverting (removing) the alternating current induced by the power transmission lines to the grounding device,
while maintaining the protective potential of the pipeline structure
