Electronic Joint Protection
Units
with Smooth
Precision Control

BZK-EPPK units are used in electrochemical corrosion protection (ECP) systems to protect underground steel structures from soil corrosion and to eliminate mutual interference between adjacent pipelines.

The unit provides accurate distribution and smooth regulation of protective
current between multiple pipelines, maintaining optimal
protective potential on each line.

Application
  • Joint protection of multiple
    pipelines powered by a single
    cathodic protection station
  • Elimination of mutual
    interference between pipelines,
    cables, and structures
  • Adjustment of current
    levels to achieve the
    required protective potential
