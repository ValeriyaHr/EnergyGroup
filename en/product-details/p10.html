Enhanced Drainage
Protection Station

A high-power station designed for intensive diversion of large stray currents, providing reliable

pipeline protection in areas with a high level of electromagnetic interference

Purpose
  • Reliable protection of steel pipelines against accelerated corrosion caused by high levels of stray currents
  • Intensive diversion of currents into a grounding system or back to their source
  • Enhancement of existing cathodic protection systems in complex sections
System Composition
  • Enhanced electrodrainage unit with cable line and regulation module
  • Contact equipment and connection cables for pipeline connection
  • High-power measurement and protection unit
  • Optional telemetry module
Key Features
  • Operation with high drainage current loads
  • Built-in high-precision modules for current and pipe-to-soil potential monitoring
  • Stable performance under conditions of increased electrical interference
  • Support for integration with telemetry systems for remote monitoring
