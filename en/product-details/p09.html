Drainage Protection
System "Polarization"

A station designed for intensive diversion of large stray currents and enhanced

pipeline protection efficiency in areas with extreme electromagnetic influence

Purpose
  • Protection of steel pipelines and utilities against corrosion caused by stray currents
  • Maintenance of optimal polarization potential required for proper operation of electrochemical corrosion protection systems
  • Diversion of unwanted currents from the pipeline with automatic operating mode control
System Composition
  • Polarization drainage unit with cable line
  • Contact equipment for connection to the structure
  • Current / potential monitoring and protection unit
  • Optional telemetry module
Key Features
  • Automatic or semi-automatic regulation depending on the
    structure-to-soil potential difference
  • Maintenance of stable protective potential,
    reducing overload of the ECP system
  • Built-in modules for drainage
    current and potential measurement
  • Ready for integration with
    telemetry systems
