Electronic
Insulating Flange
Control Units

Electronic insulating flange control units are designed for protection, monitoring, and safe discharge of electrical potential at insulating flanges in electrochemical corrosion protection (ECP) systems.

The units ensure maintenance of electrical isolation between pipeline sections during normal operation and provide automatic overvoltage diversion during emergency conditions or lightning surges

Purpose
  • Monitoring the condition of insulating flanges in ECP systems
  • Discharging accumulated potential during overvoltage events
  • Protecting insulating joints from breakdown during lightning impulses or emergency conditions
  • Ensuring electrical safety of maintenance personnel
Key Features
  • Automatic potential discharge control without loss of electrical isolation
  • Built-in lightning protection with replaceable element
  • Flange status indication (normal / fault mode)
  • Integration capability with telemetry or ECP monitoring systems
  • Compact electronic design suitable for installation in existing cabinets or control points
Applications
  • Cathodic protection systems on main pipelines
  • District heating networks
  • Oil and gas networks
  • Water supply pipelines
  • Locations with insulating flanges or monoblock installations
