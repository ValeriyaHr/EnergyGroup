Control units for insulating flanges are designed for protection, control and safe discharge of potential of electrically insulating flanges in electrochemical protection systems (ECH).

They ensure the preservation of electrical insulation between pipeline sections during normal operation of the system and automatic discharge of overvoltage in case of emergency or thunderstorm effects

Appointment
  • Control of the condition of insulating flanges in EHZ systems
  • Discharge of accumulated potential in the event of overvoltages
  • Protection of insulating joints against breakdown during lightning impulses or accidents
  • Ensuring electrical safety of personnel during maintenance
Main features
  • Automatic control of potential discharge without loss of insulation
  • Built-in lightning protection with replaceable element
  • Indication of the condition of the flange (working / emergency mode)
  • The possibility of integration into the system of telemetry or control of EHZ
  • Compact electronic design for installation in existing cabinets or control points
Scope of application
  • Cathodic protection systems on main pipelines, heat lines, oil and gas and water networks, in places where insulating flanges or monoblocks are installed
