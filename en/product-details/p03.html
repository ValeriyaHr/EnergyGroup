Magnetite
Grounding
Anodes

AZMT-type magnetite anodes are designed for cathodic protection systems of underground steel structures against corrosion.

The material — magnetite (Fe₃O₄) — is a mineral with high electronic conductivity that does not undergo anodic dissolution even at high current densities. Thanks to the technology of fusing magnetite with alloying additives in an oxygen-free environment, a material with optimal electrochemical and mechanical properties is obtained
