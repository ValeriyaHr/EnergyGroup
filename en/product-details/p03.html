Magnetite anodes of the AZMT type are intended for cathode systems
protection of steel underground structures from corrosion

The material is magnetite (Fe₃O₄), a mineral with high electronic conductivity,
which does not undergo anodic dissolution even at high current densities. Thanks to
magnetite fusion technology with alloying additives in an oxygen-free environment
a material with optimal electrochemical and mechanical properties is obtained
