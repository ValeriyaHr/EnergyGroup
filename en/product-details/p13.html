PEG COMB
Measuring Point

The PEG COMB measuring point is designed for monitoring pipe-to-soil potentials and is used in electrochemical corrosion protection systems for underground metal pipelines.

It is applied for: route measuring points, drainage measuring points (at connection locations of cathodic protection stations and additional auxiliary equipment), anode diagnostic measuring columns, installation of extended anode ground beds

Purpose
  • Monitoring of pipe-to-soil potentials
  • Installation of extended anode ground beds
System Composition
  • Terminal panel made of high-quality textolite with high dielectric and mechanical properties, heat and moisture resistance Access to the terminal panel is provided by opening the unified lock and lifting and fixing the upper part of the frame
