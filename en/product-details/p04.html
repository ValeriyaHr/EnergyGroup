Test and
Measurement Points
with BZK and Telemetry

Test and measurement points (TMP) are designed to measure
electrochemical corrosion protection parameters and to monitor
the performance of cathodic and drainage protection systems.

The telemetry-enabled version provides automated data acquisition and real-time transmission of measurements.

Functions
  • Measurement of pipe-to-soil
    potential, currents, and voltage
  • Signal acquisition from multiple
    underground utilities (in
    joint protection systems)
  • Online data transmission to
    the telemetry system
  • Monitoring the condition of protective
    devices and ECP parameters
Configuration
  • TMP enclosure with
    sealed terminal connections
  • Telemetry module for
    data collection and transmission
    (GSM or radio channel)
  • Connection terminals for
    reference electrodes,
    pipelines, and anodes
Applications
  • Main oil and gas pipelines
