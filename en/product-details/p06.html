Telemetry and Remote
Control System for Cathodic
Protection Stations

A remote monitoring and control system designed for online supervision of operating parameters and remote adjustment of cathodic protection station modes without the need for on-site visits.

The system ensures reliable, real-time control of cathodic protection effectiveness
