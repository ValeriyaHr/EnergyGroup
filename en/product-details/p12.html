Anode Ground Current
Monitoring Device

The AGCM device is used in electrochemical corrosion protection systems
for extended underground metal structures (pipelines) and is designed
for the distribution and monitoring of protective currents of anode grounding systems.

The device provides connection of anode ground conductors to the positive
pole of the cathodic protection station and enables full compliance
with proper anode ground connection requirements

Versions
  • AGCM
  • Based on terminal connections and current-measuring shunts for connecting cables from anode grounds combined on a common busbar, with connection to the positive pole of the cathodic protection station.
  • AGCM.M1
  • Based on terminal connections and current-measuring shunts with the option to connect individual balancing resistors for each anode channel, with connection to the positive pole of the cathodic protection station.

Applications
  • Main and distribution pipelines
  • District heating networks and underground utilities
  • Medium- and high-pressure gas pipelines
  • Cathodic protection systems for extended metal structures
  • Anode fields with variable or uneven current distribution
  • Facilities with multiple independent cathodic protection circuits
