Advantages

  • Ability to balance and equalize currents in channels

Current monitoring device
anode grounding devices

PKZA is used in systems of electrochemical protection of long lines
of underground metal objects (pipelines) from corrosion and intended
for the distribution and control of protective currents of anode earthing devices.

The device ensures the connection of anode ground conductors to the positive pole of the cathodic protection station and allows you to fulfill the requirements for the correct connection of the anode grounding as efficiently as possible

Modifications
  • PKZA
  • Made on the basis of terminal connections and current-measuring shunts for connecting cables from anode grounding devices, united by one bus, with connection to the positive pole of the cathodic protection station
  • PKZA. M1
  • Made on the basis of terminal connections and current-measuring shunts for connecting cables from anode grounding devices, united by one bus, with the possibility of connecting individual balancing resistors, with connection to the positive pole of the cathodic protection station
