ANALYSIS OF THE OBJECT AND
TECHNICAL TASK

The client provides basic data about the facility (diagrams, operating conditions, soil parameters). The company's specialists conduct technical analysis, calculate currents and form technically a justified task (TOR) with an optimal composition of equipment

DEVELOPMENT OF THE SOLUTION AND
PRODUCTION

On the basis of the agreed TOR, an individual project of the EHZU system is developed, including all key elements — anodes, stations cathodic protection, BZK blocks, measurement points, telemetry — manufactured in-house in compliance with DSTU and ISO

CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION
EXECUTIVE WORKS

After the equipment is manufactured, installation, connection and commissioning are carried out by Prime Energy Group engineers. System is put into operation with the verification of parameters and preparation of technical documentation

MONITORING
AND SERVICE

After putting the system into operation, technical support is provided: diagnostics of anode fields and stations, maintenance of electronic modules, remote monitoring via GSM telemetry This ensures the stability of potentials and prolongs the facility's operational life
