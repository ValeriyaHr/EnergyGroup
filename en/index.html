[ Realization of projects

We follow a clear and structured workflow —

Each stage is supervised by specialized engineers, ensuring accuracy, reliability, and full technical compliance
Facility Analysis and
Technical Specification

The client provides baseline data on the facility (drawings, operating conditions, and soil parameters). The company’s specialists perform a technical assessment, calculate the required protection currents, and develop a technically substantiated Technical Specification (TS) with an optimized equipment configuration

Solution Design and
Manufacturing

Based on the approved specification, an individual electrochemical corrosion protection system is designed. Key components — anodes, cathodic protection stations, joint protection units, telemetry systems — are manufactured in-house in compliance with DSTU and ISO standards

Cathodic Protection Construction
and Installation Works

Following equipment manufacturing, installation, electrical connection, and commissioning are carried out by Prime Energy Group engineers. The system is put into operation with verification of operating parameters and completion of the required technical documentation

Monitoring
and Service

After commissioning, continuous technical support is provided: diagnostics of anode ground beds and stations, maintenance of electronic modules, remote monitoring via GSM telemetry. This ensures stable protective potential sand extends the operational life of the facility
